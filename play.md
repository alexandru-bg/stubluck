StubLuck — How to Play

Start with $500. Guess right, get rich. Guess wrong, go broke.

The Button (one button, three gestures)

Tap → HIGHER
Hold → LOWER
Double-tap → CASH OUT
Each Round

An ante is taken from your balance and placed on the table as the POT ($50 rising to $500 as your balance grows).
A ticket is dealt with a number 1–99.
Guess if the next ticket will be higher or lower.
Correct → pot doubles, streak +1, new ticket
Wrong → pot lost, new round
Tie → push, pot and streak survive, new ticket
Cash out any time to bank the pot into your BAL.
Edge Cases

Ticket = 1 → only HIGHER works
Ticket = 99 → only LOWER works
How You Lose
Balance hits $0 and you lose the next round → GAME OVER.

How You Score
Your score is your highest balance this session — shown as HI in the top-right. Only cashed-out winnings count. Bank it before you bust.

Ticket Color (your risk meter)

Range	Border
1–25	red
26–50	gold
51–75	green
76–99	navy

Pro tip: double-tap when you're ahead. A $400 cash-out beats a $3,200 pot that you never take home.
